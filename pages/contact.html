Frequently asked questions

What types of companies do you work with?

We primarily partner with educational publishers (K-12, secondary, vocational), EdTech companies scaling or modernizing platforms, school groups, ministries, and NGOs in education. Our typical contacts are product owners, CTOs, chief learning officers, and innovation teams.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. We focus on delivering value incrementally rather than big-bang releases. A typical engagement starts with a discovery phase, followed by iterative development cycles. We're happy to discuss specifics once we understand your needs.

Can you work with our existing technology stack?

Absolutely. We have experience with a wide range of technologies and can integrate with existing systems. Our approach to modernization emphasizes step-by-step improvement rather than disruptive rewrites.

What makes your AI solutions different?

Our AI is built for education — not just added to it. We focus on curriculum fidelity (not hallucination), Socratic-style guidance that strengthens reasoning, and tools that help publishers improve their content based on real learning data. We believe in AI that supports learning, not shortcuts it.
